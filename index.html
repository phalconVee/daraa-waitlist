Questions we hear most

I'm a solo stylist / booth renter — is this for me?

Yes. Daraa works for a single chair just as well as a 15-stylist shop. The $79/month covers one chair, or one location. If you're renting a booth, you're probably our best-fit early user — you keep 100% of the retail uplift.

What exactly does my $1 get me?

A reserved spot as one of our five founding Atlanta pilot users — solo stylists and salons alike. When we launch in the next 60 days, your pricing is locked at $79/month for 24 months. Change your mind before then, we refund the dollar.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Daraa is a website, not an app. You log in once on your phone via a text link and stay logged in. Your clients don't install anything either — they just get a text.

How does Daraa know what products I stock?

You upload a simple spreadsheet of your inventory (SKU, product, price) — takes five minutes the first time. We match it to our ingredient database. Updates are weekly via re-upload, or automatic if you're on Shopify or Square (on the Core plan).

What if the recommendation is wrong?

You always have the final say. Daraa generates the routine in seconds; you review, swap, or approve before it's sent. Nothing reaches your client without your sign-off.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, no questions asked. Your founding pricing only applies while your subscription is active.
